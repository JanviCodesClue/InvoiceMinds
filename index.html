Who We Serve

We specialize exclusively in supporting cannabis businesses across the United States. From dispensaries and cultivation facilities to multi–location operators, we deliver accounting and financial reporting tailored to the unique requirements of the cannabis industry, and our framework also adapts seamlessly to other highly regulated industries.

Dispensaries
Accounting, cash controls, daily sales reporting, and COGS guidance.
Cultivation & Grow Facilities
Cost tracking, inventory controls, and production analysis.
Multi–Location Operators (MSOs)
Consolidated reporting, compliance-focused workflows, and standardized processes.
