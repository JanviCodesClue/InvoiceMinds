About Invoice Minds

A specialized accounting partner empowering cannabis businesses to navigate 280E compliance, optimize operations, and scale with confidence.

Our Story

Invoice Minds was founded with a simple purpose — to provide cannabis businesses with accurate, dependable financial management built on trust, clarity, and compliance. Our roots span Belgium, India, and the United States, allowing us to combine strong European processes, skilled Indian accounting talent, and a deep understanding of U.S. cannabis regulations.
